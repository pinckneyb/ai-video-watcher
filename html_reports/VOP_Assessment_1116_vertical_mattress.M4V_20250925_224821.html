Summative Assessment:
The operator executes the vertical mattress technique with reliable instrument control, consistent pass symmetry, and uniform spacing, producing good epidermal eversion and sturdy tissue apposition. Needle handling and forceps use are precise and atraumatic, and knots are routinely well seated and trimmed. Tension management is satisfactory overall, with deep and superficial components working together to support the closure; however, occasional localized overtightening produced minor puckering where stitches clustered. Workflow and economy of motion are excellent, reflecting efficient, deliberate technique suitable for clinical closure with only minor refinement needed to eliminate focal over-tensioning.
