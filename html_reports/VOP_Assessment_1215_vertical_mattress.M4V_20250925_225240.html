Summative Assessment:
The operator demonstrates competent to proficient execution of vertical mattress technique across the treated incision. Passes are symmetrical and well oriented, spacing is uniform, and the tactile balance between deeper support and superficial approximation produces consistent mild eversion without focal puckering. Instrument control is steady and bimanual coordination is efficient, supporting precise needle arcs and predictable suture placement. Knot security appears reliable with controlled tail management and consistent tightening, though specific throw detail is not fully observable. Overall the performance reflects a strong, methodical technique suitable for superficial skin closure with good cosmetic alignment; minor refinement in explicit knot‑tying visibility and maintaining exclusive forceps use rather than occasional fingertip substitution would further optimize reproducibility under operative conditions.
