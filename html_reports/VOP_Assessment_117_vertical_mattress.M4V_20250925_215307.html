Summative Assessment:
The closure demonstrates a methodical, proficient vertical mattress technique with consistent needle control, precise tissue handling, and uniform spacing. Layered pass execution produced dependable edge approximation and maintained intended eversion, while knot tying and tail management were routinely secure. Areas for refinement include more consistent tension modulation to avoid localized puckering and the small residual gap near the superior stitch; deliberate, slightly gentler superficial tightening in that region would improve contour. Overall performance is competent and efficient, suitable for clinical closure with minor attention to tension balance to reach excellence.
