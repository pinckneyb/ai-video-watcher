Summative Assessment:
The operator demonstrated a competent, reproducible vertical mattress technique with controlled needle handling, precise edge eversion, and uniform spacing. Needle arcs were predictable and instrument coordination was tight, resulting in consistent dermal purchase and reliable epidermal apposition throughout the suture line. Knots were secure and tails appropriately trimmed. Tension balance between the deep and superficial components was well managed—deep support established without excessive epidermal strangulation—producing a cosmetically acceptable, slightly everted closure. Workflow was efficient with compact hand positioning; minor, brief attentional shifts to adjacent tasks occurred but did not compromise the overall technical quality. Overall performance meets a high standard of competence with only marginal room for refinement in minimizing occasional ancillary movements.
