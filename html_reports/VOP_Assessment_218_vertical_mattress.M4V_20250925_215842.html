Summative Assessment:
The operator demonstrates a proficient vertical mattress closure with consistent pass symmetry, controlled needle handling, and precise tissue stabilization. Sutures are placed at regular intervals with reliable perpendicular entry and exit, producing uniform eversion and good epidermal approximation. Knot technique is competent—instrument-tied throws are secure and advanced appropriately with tidy tail management. Tension distribution between the deep and superficial components is balanced, providing structural support without over-tightening the skin. Hand and instrument economy is strong overall, with only occasional brief departures to exchange tools; these do not compromise closure quality. Overall performance is competent and efficient, though continued attention to maintaining the needle grip at optimal positions and minimizing instrument exchanges will further refine speed and consistency.
