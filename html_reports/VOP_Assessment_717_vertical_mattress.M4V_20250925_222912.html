Summative Assessment:
The operator demonstrates a proficient vertical mattress technique with controlled needle arcs, precise forceps counter‑traction, and deliberate interrupted placement producing consistent edge eversion and secure knots. Pass symmetry and spacing are uniform along the worked superior-to-midline segment, tensioning is measured with deep support evident and superficial closure avoiding overcompression, and instrument handling is economical and coordinated. Primary deficiency is incomplete coverage of the full incision: the inferior portion remains gaping at the end of the recording, indicating an interrupted workflow or incomplete progression rather than poor stitch quality where placed. Overall competence is strong for the executed segment — technique is reproducible, tissue handling is gentle, knots are secure, and eversion and spacing meet expected standards.
