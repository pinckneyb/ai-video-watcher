Summative Assessment:
The surgeon executed the vertical mattress line with disciplined, bimanual technique producing consistent dermal purchase, reliable edge apposition, and predictable eversion. Needle handling and forceps control were precise, with small, efficient motions and appropriate adjustments as the field became crowded with previous sutures. Knot formation and trimming were secure and uniform, and tensioning demonstrated good balance between deeper support and superficial approximation without skin strangulation or significant puckering. Overall this is a competent, proficient vertical mattress performance with minor, acceptable variations in bite depth and spacing that do not compromise the integrity of the closure.
