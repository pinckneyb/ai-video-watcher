Summative Assessment:
The operator performed a methodical, well-controlled vertical mattress closure. Needle handling, forceps use, and instrument choreography were precise, producing uniform spacing, reliable knots, and consistent slight eversion across the entire incision. Tension distribution between the supportive deep component and the superficial approximation was appropriate, preventing gaping while avoiding excessive epidermal tension. Overall competence is strong with efficient, repeatable technique and sound tissue handling.
