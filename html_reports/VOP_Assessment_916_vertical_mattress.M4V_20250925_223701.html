Summative Assessment:
The operator performed a competent vertical mattress closure with controlled needle handling, reliable forceps use, and consistent stitch geometry. The series of bites produced uniform approximation and visible eversion, with deep support and superficial approximation balanced to avoid epidermal strangulation. Knotwork was attentive and secure, with proper management of tails.
