2. Gentle tissue handling

7. Economy of time and motion
Motion economy is strong: hands and instruments remain close to the field with efficient needle passes, tight bimanual coordination, and minimal unnecessary movements off the work area. Workflow is rhythmic and time‑efficient.

Summative Assessment:
The operator executed a competent vertical mattress closure with consistent pass symmetry, controlled needle handling, and reliable tissue presentation. Deep and superficial components function together to provide firm support and precise epidermal approximation; spacing and perpendicular entry were uniform along the entire line. Eversion is deliberate and appropriate, and there is no evidence of undue tension, inversion, or edge necrosis. Knot work and overall workflow are efficient and secure, with atraumatic forceps use and tight bimanual coordination throughout. The performance demonstrates proficient, reproducible technique suitable for clinical application, though a few knots and throws could be scrutinized for the absolute highest standard of excellence.
